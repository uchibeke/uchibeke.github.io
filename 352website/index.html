TESTING METHODOLOGY

5 RFID cards and 1 RFID key fob are tested 10 times intially to see whether they all work and to provide a base case (control) to compare the results of the with the material around them.

The RFID reader will read the cards and the ID of the card will be printed to a spreadsheet to record the attempts that the reader was able to penetrate the material and read the card.

All of the materials tested are things which can be found within a ordinary household. The RFID card and RFID fob are each wrapped in one of the materials and will be tested like in the control.
