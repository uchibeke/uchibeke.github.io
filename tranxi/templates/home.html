About

Tranxi or UTranslate is inspired by Jungle Language, a language we often resorted to as high school kids in Port harcourt, to conceal what we are talking about. I had to buy lunch for one of my classmates, Divine, to learn the language. I did not, however, realize how easy the language was.

Basically, in Jungle language, the vowels are changed to numbers- a = 1, e = 2, i = 3, o = 4, and u = 5. Each of the consonants, on the other hand, remain as is, however, an 'a' is appended to them - B becomes Ba, C becomes Ca, D becomes Da, F becomes Fa ... and Z becomes Za.

In Jungle Language, if I wanted to write How are You?, I would say Ha4Wa 1Ra2 Ya45?

In jungle language, we also say out each alpabet when speaking, so; How are You?, would be said as Ha-4-Wa 1-Ra-2 Ya-4-5?, pronoucing each consonant and vowel. (◕‿◕)

Tranxi's default language is Jungle language. However, with Tranxi, you can also create you own language just like Jungle language. Just add what you want each of the alphabets to be, start typing and whatever you type gets translated. Copy and send via email. Click the share button to share using most mediums.
