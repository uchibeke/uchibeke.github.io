Wedding Planning is stressful. Make it Fulfilling with Lush Plans

May 11, 2018

Temidele Kareem

Photo by Tessa Rampersad on Unsplash

With planning a Nigerian wedding or any wedding at all, there’ll always be more things to do even when you think you have done the most. Planning a wedding involves putting so many ideas together. These ideas entail: a venue, event planner, caterer, DJ, photographer and so on. The individuals or groups that provide these services are what most people refer to as vendors. Well, specifically, wedding vendors.

Photo by Annie Spratt on Unsplash

There are only two of you walking down the aisle eventually but the amount of people that will assist in the perfectness of that dream are far more profuse. Your vendors are the experts who will manifest your wedding reverie. Problem is, with a wide range of varying options, one could consider it grueling to search for the best wedding vendors in your area. This is where Lush Plans turns your stress into fulfilment.

Lush Plans is an event management app with more than 13,000 vendors ranked by previous jobs and budgets, created to ease the process of managing and planning the perfect weddings.

As intending couples, you are presented with a domain to design your e-invitation with your custom message, color and background animations which would be sent out to all your guests by email or SMS. Lush Plans provides an avenue to manage your own wedding by adding details such as guests, tracking RSVPs and gifts.

Most importantly, Lush Plans furnishes a platform that soothes the burden of setting a budget and building your wedding list by delivering vendor recommendations to match your budget and list.

The most significant information as regards the vendors are meticulously integrated on the website thereby lubricating the planning process for the bride and groom-to-be.

So you get to save, own it and make your wedding rock!

Your wedding is a very special day and every couple deserves the most optimum result. Lush Plans puts in all the effort to achieve whatever requirements you have while working according to them. Start your search now for the most suitable Nigerian wedding venues, Nigerian wedding cakes, decorators/florists, photographers and more for you!

Visit www.lushplans.com
