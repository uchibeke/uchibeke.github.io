UGradr Android App


Inspired by my obsessive compulsion for the continuous and frequent calculation and estimation of my grades. ᕦ(ò_óˇ)ᕤ

Problem:

  1. I needed an app that could calulate and estimate of my grade in a stupidly quick and dirty way.
  2. I wanted the app to to be intuitive and the experience fluid.
  3. I wanted an app that was fast, lightweight and customizable.
  4. The only feature I wanted was the ability to enter my scores and getting my grade or estimated grade back.

See it in action at gradr.uchibeke.com.

Strategy and goals:

  1. Made with Angular JS on Android Studio.
  2. Started an a saturday morning by learning Angular JS on lynda.com. Then I drew out the layout of the app.
  3. Made changes to the sketch and then started the making the app starting with a a basic webapp interface.
  4. I actually first made sure all calculations were implemented and that they worked correctly.
  5. I then implemented the UI and, next, I optimized it for mobile.
  6. For each change I made, I conducted unit tests to for each functionality
  7. Completed a working version by 3am on Monday. This is an unfinished project but I am currently using it on my phone.

Do check it out at UGradr
