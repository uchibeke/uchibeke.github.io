Uchi Uchibeke is the Founder of NaijaHacks, Nigeria's biggest tech invention competition. He pioneered a Blockchain Client Identity Management Ecosystem at the Royal Bank of Canada (RBC) and a Blockchain access control Ecosystem for big data security which is published in an Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) paper.

Uchi's work is inspired by his experience growing up in Nigeria and his desire to inspire the next generation of Nigerians to be leaders, makers, and disruptors. Uchi currently works as a Software Developer at Shopify.
