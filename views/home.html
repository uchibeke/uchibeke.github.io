TRANXI

When I was a boy growing up in Nigeria, my father introduced me via a record to classical music and meditation. He was happy I picked this up, however, he used to get mad at me to speaking in "Jungle Language", a language that we highschool kids discovered. Tranxi is inspired by my experience and my desire to help people create their own languages.

ONFARM

Farmers value firsthand feedback from their peers about crop varieties that their peers have tried. The farmers we talked to value these feedbacks more than ratings in a Seed Guide published by a Corporation or by the Government. Onfarm is born from the feedback we got from farmers about the Sasktachewan Seed Guide.
