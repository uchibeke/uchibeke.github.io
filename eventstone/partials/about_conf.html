CONFERENCE


This page contains only information to help you manage conferences and other formal events. Get help with organizing Parties .

Evewhyte will help you organize your party. With Evewhyte, you can generate and design tickets, check-in people as they arrive at the door and even keep track of the people who attend your event and how much money you made.

HOW TO

Start in the Add Attendee page. Copy and paste the list of attendees from an excel file or add them one at a time, one par line in the text field.

Next, go to the Design Name Badge page, enter the event details and select the name badge style and color. Each guest you added will have one name badge generated automatically for them.

Print the name badges.

You can now check in attendees in the Live screen page. Type the name or other detail of the attendee and click "check-in".
