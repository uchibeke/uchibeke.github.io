This page contains only information to help you keeping track of event attendees by signing them in as they arrive. Get help with organizing Formal Events or Parties and Bashes.
Evewhyte will help you keep track of people who attend your meeting, event or class. With Evewhyte, you can let people sign in at to your class or event. You can either sign them in at the door or pass an ipad around to allow them to sign in.


HOW TO

Start in the Live page.

Select the option to Allow New people to add their name and click go.

Get a summary of the event as more people checkin and in the Attendee page after the event.
