Unevenly Even


“Unevenly Even – Party or Revenge” is a short film inspired by a dream. It is a mystery thriller which subtly showcases the hype of college life and quickly turns in to a crime thriller as it reaches its climax. It is proposed to be shot in a found-footage, Cinéma vérité style, where actors break the fourth wall.

SYNOPSIS

“During final exams, six college seniors get a similar, anonymous text about a secret, off-the-record party, deep in the woods. They impulsively agree to attend this secret party, only to discover it is their friend from freshman year. However, he is no longer in St. Andrews for disciplinary reasons, and now he has come back to revenge. Revenge for what? What happens after this? Do they live to tell the story?”
